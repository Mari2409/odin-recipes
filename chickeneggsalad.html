Chicken and Egg Salad

This is a mouth-watering,high in protein,healthy and no sweet stuff meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups diced cooked chicken
  • 3 hard-boiled eggs,diced
  • 1/2 onion,chopped
  • 1 stalk celery,diced
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise,or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 pinch salt and cracked black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine chicken,eggs,onion,celory,mayonnaise,cilantro,parsley,salt and pepper in a big bowl;stir well.
  2. Taste and adjust salt,pepper and mayonnaise to your liking.
  3. Place in the refridgerator to chill,about 3 hours
