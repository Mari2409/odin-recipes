Cheesy Casserole

A hearty casserole has bacon,eggs,hash browns,and three different cheeses.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sliced bacon,diced
  • 1 sweet onion,chopped
  • 4 cups frozen shredded hash brown potatoes thawed
  • 9 eggs,lightly beaten
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups small and cottage cheese
  • 1 1/4 cups shredded Swiss cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C).
  2. Heat a large skilled over medium-high heat;cook and stir bacon and until bacon is evenly browned,about 10 minutes.Drain.Transfer bacon and onion to a large bowl.Stir in potatoes,eggs,Cheddar cheese,cottage chees and Swiss cheese.Pour mixture into prepared baking dish.
  3. Bake in preheat oven until eggs are set and cheese is melted,45 to 50 minutes.Let stand 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
